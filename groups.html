Groups Overview

Groups are a setting where we can work together to live out Artisanâ€™s values in a smaller, more intimate context. We have three types of groups that accomplish this goal in different ways:

  • Our home groups are our most common type of groups. They tend to be ongoing groups where people get to know each other more deeply through sharing, prayer, and reading the Bible together.
  • Our connection groups are occasional, short-term gatherings where people spend time together doing something fun, such as watching movies, playing games, brewing beer, or sampling foods.
  • Our study groups are where we go deeper in studying scripture, theology, spiritual disciplines, and culture. Typically led by a person with some expertise and/or knowledge, study groups happen a few times per year.
