Home Groups

Artisan small groups are a way for us to live out the mission and values of our church in a smaller, more relational setting, where it is easier to explore faith and spirituality at your own pace. We would love to have you get involved with one today! Most of our groups are open to new members; check the group list for more information on getting involved and the contact person for each group.
