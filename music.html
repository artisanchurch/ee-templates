Music

We love modern music and employ lots of different styles, with most of our arrangements landing somewhere between rock and alt-folk. We currently have three different bands that play on a scheduled rotation. Guest musicians and impromptu lineups are common.

We also love the theological and lyrical depth of hymns, both old and new. Our worship repertoire is made up of classic hymns and the best modern songs of faith.

And finally, we engage music as an art form that helps us encounter God, not just with its textual content but also with its sonic beauty. Sometimes, worship means listening as well as singing.
