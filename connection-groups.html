Second Friday Game Night

Second Friday of each month, 7:00 pm

Pull out your board games, your dice, and your cards and bring them down to Artisan. Make some new friends while you learn a new game. 7:00 pm ’til we’re done; BYO snacks to share.

Men’s Group

Once a month; day and location varies

The men of Artisan church get together once a month for fun and fellowship. Current and past activities include playing lawn games, visiting breweries, bowling, and watching movies together.

Join our Facebook group to be kept abreast of upcoming men’s events!
