Giving

Donate quickly and securely using our online giving service, hosted at InFellowship.com.

If you’ve never used this system before, it’s simple to sign up.

  1. Create an account for yourself. (InFellowship accounts are for individuals, but don’t worry, your donation will roll into your household donation totals.)
  2. Provide your checking account or card information and specify whether you want to give once or set up a recurring donation.

Benefits

There are several benefits to online giving for both you and Artisan.

Simplicity
No more forgetting to bring your cash or check on Sunday morning, or forgetting to mail your gift or catch up later if you miss a week.
Security
Online giving is more secure than giving by cash or check in that an electronic gift can’t be lost or stolen.
Online Statements
Creating an account with InFellowship allows you to log in and view your household’s giving—whether you donated online or at church.
Helping Artisan
Online contributions require less processing from our administrative staff, since the information is logged and deposited automatically.

Our Policies

Your information is held securely by the InFellowship system. This information will remain private and will never be given away to third parties, other than to process your scheduled contributions.

You can schedule new contributions at any time and can modify or cancel those contributions at any time prior to the date they are scheduled. Once the contribution has been processed, however, it cannot be canceled.

Need help?

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our giving manager, Scott Cranfill, at giving@artisanchurch.com.

You can also use the contact form inside the InFellowship system, or browse their help pages. Once logged in, the "Contact us" and "Help" links are in the bar at the top of the page.
