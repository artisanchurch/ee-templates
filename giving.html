Giving

Donate quickly and securely using our online giving service, hosted at CCBChurch.com.

If you’ve never used this system before, it’s simple to sign up.

  1. Create an account for yourself. (Church Community Builder accounts are for individuals, but don’t worry, your donation will roll into your household donation totals.)
  2. Provide your checking account or card information and specify whether you want to give once or set up a recurring donation.

Benefits

There are several benefits to online giving for both you and Artisan.

Simplicity
You will not need to remember if you gave this month or need to mail offering checks when you are out of town.
Security
Online giving is more secure than giving by cash or check, because an electronic gift can’t be lost or stolen.
Online Statements
Creating an account with Church Community Builder allows you to log in and view your household’s giving—whether you donated online or at church.
Helping Artisan
Online contributions require less processing from our administrative staff, since the information is logged and deposited automatically.

Our Policies

Your information is held securely by the Church Community Builder system. This information will remain private and will never be given away to third parties, other than to process your scheduled contributions.

You can schedule new contributions at any time, and can modify or cancel those contributions at any time prior to the date they are scheduled. Once the contribution has been processed, however, it cannot be canceled.

Need help?

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our giving manager, Scott Cranfill, at giving@artisanchurch.com.

Why give to the church?

In one sense, giving to Artisan might be similar to giving to any other non-profit: if you believe in our mission and our calling, you should give! 100% of our funding comes from the generosity of people who believe in our ministry. But as people of faith, we also consider giving to the church to be an act of worship – sharing the gifts that God has given us with the larger community he is building in and through us. Thanking God for his provision in our lives, we pray that those who give would be shaped by their generous sacrifice, and that he would bless and multiply the gifts that are given in such a way that we would see his kingdom become a reality on earth as it is in heaven.
